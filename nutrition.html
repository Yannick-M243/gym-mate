Nutrition

I always say nutrition is the key to success, let’s say nutrition is about 70% of the results you will get. That’s why it is important to know how to eat according to your body type. I you eat well you are halfway there.

For now, I will be providing advice only for the two people's most common goals, which are fat loss/shredding, and muscle gain/bulking.

Fat loss / Shredding

To lose fat, you must avoid foods containing fat, soft drinks (sodas, juices, beers, …) sauces (ketchup, mayonnaise, ...), eat smaller quantities, and cook with a minimum of oil (olive or coconut oil), avoid salt and spices containing salt. The fewer calories you eat the more likely you are to lose weight. You can stay on 3 meals a day basis but make sure to eat smaller quantities and clean food. What I mean by clean food is: a good source of protein (fish, chicken, beef, egg white), green vegetables and carbs (In small quantity).


Losing fat is different than shredding. When you are shredding you must make sure to have enough protein and more carbs in your food to avoid muscle loss.
