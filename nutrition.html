Nutrition

I always say nutrition is the key to success, let’s say nutrition is about 70% of the results you will get. That’s why it is important to know how to eat according to your body type. If you eat well you are halfway there.

For now, I will be providing advice only for the two people's most common goals, which are fat loss/shredding, and muscle gain/bulking.

Fat loss / Shredding

While shredding you must:
  • Avoid foods containing fat
  • Avoid soft drinks (sodas, juices, beers, …)
  • Avoid sauces (ketchup, mayonnaise, ...)
  • Eat smaller quantities
  • Cook with a minimum of oil (olive or coconut oil)
  • Avoid salt and spices containing salt.

The fewer calories you eat the more likely you are to lose weight. You can stay on 3 meals a day basis but make sure to eat smaller quantities and clean food. What I mean by clean food is: a good source of protein (fish, chicken, beef, egg white), green vegetables and carbs (In small quantity)


Losing fat is different than shredding. When you are shredding you must make sure to have enough protein and more carbs in your food to avoid muscle loss.

Endomorph Mesomorph Ectomorph
Losing fat or getting shredded for someone who has that body type is the most challenging thing, I require a zero-tolerance diet. To lose weight as an endomorph you will have to eat very clean. For the most motivated I recommend eating intermittent fasting with 2 meals per day. To have more information on intermittent fasting click Here. For mesomorphs losing fat might not require the same intensity as endomorphs but they might require more efforts to shred. Ectomorphs are less susceptible to the need to lose weight but if they have to, they should do it effortlessly.

Gaining muscle / Bulking

While bulking you:
  • Neet to eat way more protein
  • Neet to eat more carbs
  • Neet to avoid sauces (ketchup, mayonnaise, ...)
  • Have to eat a bigger food quantity
  • Do not have to be too vigilant about the way you cook(normal oil quantity)
  • Can eat healthy fat

By too vigilant I do not mean you can eat anything you want, try to eat clean contrary to what some people say. Use oil or coconut oil to cook, avoid sauces, and make sure to add vegetables to your meals

Most of the time gaining muscle comes with gaining fat too because muscle gain workout requires more energy, and that energy comes with the food you eat. To gain muscle some people eat 5 times per day, but I recommend 4 times per day (each 3-4 hours).

Endomorph Mesomorph Ectomorph
Endomorphs can bulk easily but they are most likely to get more fat while bulking and following a muscle gaining diet. If you are an endomorph and still want to gain muscles without getting fat, I would recommend paying more attention to the way you cook (less oil, salt, butter), the choice of your food, and the quantity of the carbs you eat. For mesomorphs bulking is less challenging than the two other body types if you eat accordingly. Bulking is very challenging for ectomorphs, the success of their muscle gain will hardly depend on how much and what they eat. If you are motivated, I would recommend you to eat 5 times a day (every 3 hours), but remember to choose what you eat wisely.
