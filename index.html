Welcome to Gym Mate !

What's Gym mate ?

As the name says itself, Gym Mate is a website which takes place to assists people who want to start training as their virtual gym mate. Gym mate helps beginners with some training and nutrition advice based on my own experience, to help them reach their goals. Starting training is not only about getting a gym membership, to get started you must know a few important things that won't make you waste your time at the gym.

Before Getting Started

Once you have a goal, the first thing you need to get started is to know your body, you cannot eat and train the same way as someone who does not have the same body type as you. To get results you must eat and train according to your body type.

What's body type?

My nutrion and training advice are based on 3 body types (morphotypes) Ectomorph, Endomorph and Mesomorph
