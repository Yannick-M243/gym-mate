About me

Who am I?

My name is Yannick, I am passionate about gym/fitness since I was young. I have about 8 years of experience in the field, and during those years I have been trained by many differents coaches including international professional bodybuilder, I have trained for many different goals and reached them during my fitness journey(fat loss, shredding, bulking, and more), I have also tried and followed a lot of diet plans and nutrition programs.

Experience

I don't have any certification as a professional personal trainer but I have the passion and years of experience. I have never tried to make money as a personal trainer or coach because I have other plans for the futur but I have trained few people who showed impressive results by training with me and following the personalized diet plans I made for them. Even if can't train people at the gym, I am constantly providing them with good advice, this is my way of sharing my experience on the side and pursuing my other goals.

Notice: All advices form this website are based on my personel experience, your are free to apply them or not.
